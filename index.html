Simple Mirror

Simple Mirror is a utility based on rsync to create and update back-up copies in few clicks.

  • Choose an origin folder;
  • Connect an external drive as the destination;
  • Synchronize to turn the destination into a mirror of the origin.

The origin and destination folders must be correctly specified. If not, THERE WILL BE DATA LOSS!

Find out more about how rsync works in this video.
