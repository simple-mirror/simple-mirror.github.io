Simple Mirror

Welcome!

Simple Mirror is a reliable rsync wrapper to create and update back-up copies in one click.

  • Choose an origin folder;
  • Connect a back-up medium as the destination;
  • Synchronize to turn the destination into a mirror of the origin.

Find out more about how rsync works in this video.
